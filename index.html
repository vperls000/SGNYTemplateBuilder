The Siegel+Gale template builder allows you to choose, customize and download Microsoft Office templates — agenda, email signature, envelope, fax, label, letter and PowerPoint. Using pull-down menus, you can select the item, size, city and even enter personal information (such as your name and contact information) to be pre-populated in some templates. After downloading to your computer, you can then use the template in Office as usual.
